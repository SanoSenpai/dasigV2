ABOUT

Dasig is a Visayan word that means "to inspire, encourage, hold on." It is something that everyone of us needs right now. In these trying times, everyone is in constant battle, but with this simple advocacy, we want our organization to be of help in the simplest possible way.

With a fervent commitment to this simple yet powerful advocacy, our organization seeks to extend a helping hand in the most fundamental manner possible. We aspire to be agents of change, catalysts for positivity, and providers of solace to those who find themselves struggling amidst the chaos.

In our endeavor to make a tangible difference, we call upon the generosity and compassion of our community. By joining hands and pooling our resources, we can reach out to those who remain unseen and unheard, offering them the support and assistance they so desperately need.

We humbly appeal to your benevolence to aid us in our mission. Your contributions, no matter how modest, will serve as a lifeline for those who are yet to be reached. Every donation, every act of kindness, is a testament to the indomitable spirit of humanity. Together, let us sow the seeds of Dasig, nurturing hope and resilience in the hearts of those who need it most.

Here For You

If you are in need of food, hygiene kits, cash assistance, or clothes it is our mission to support and assist. We will give our best to cater your needs. We have a lot of good quality clothes that has been donated by our generous donors.

Volunteer With Us

Every December or whenever there is a big project, we need volunteers that will help us distribute the goods, organize, collect and deliver the donations. If you are interested to join our team, please drop a message on our Facebook page.

Fund Our Project

A monetary gift from you can save lives and help the most vulnerable. Donations enable us to provide immediate assistance to those people who are in need. It secures financial aid that can be used by the people in need. Every donation goes a long way.
